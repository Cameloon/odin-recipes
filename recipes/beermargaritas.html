Peanut Butter & Chia Berry Jam English Muffin

Description:

The addition of chia seeds in the quick “jam” topping adds heart-healthy omega-3s to this healthy breakfast recipe.

    Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsweetened mixed frozen berries
  • 2 teaspoons natural peanut butt
  • 1 whole-wheat English muffin, toasted

    Directions:

  1. Microwave berries in a medium microwave-safe bowl for 30 seconds;
    stir and microwave 30 seconds more. Stir in chia seeds.
  2. Spread peanut butter on the English muffin. Top with the berry-chia mixture.
